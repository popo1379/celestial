A natal chart is your cosmic blueprint — fixed at birth. But the planets keep moving. **Transits** are the live positions of planets in today's sky, and the angles they make to your natal placements describe the weather of your life right now.

Understanding transits turns astrology from a personality test into a timing tool.

## 1. Transit Speed: Which Planet, Which Timeline

Each planet moves at a different speed, so each one governs a different layer of timing:

| Planet | Time in a sign | What it governs |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| Moon | 2.5 days | Daily moods, instincts |
| Mercury | ~14–30 days | Communication, thinking |
| Venus | ~23 days–4 months | Love, money, pleasure |
| Mars | ~6–7 weeks | Energy, drive, conflict |
| Jupiter | ~1 year | Growth, opportunity |
| Saturn | ~2.5 years | Structure, responsibility |
| Uranus | ~7 years | Change, disruption |
| Neptune | ~14 years | Dreams, dissolution |
| Pluto | ~12–30 years | Transformation |

## 2. The Three Layers of Transit Timing

Think of transits as three nested cycles:

1. **Daily weather**: Moon, Mercury, Venus transits shape your week
2. **Monthly chapters**: Mars, Jupiter transits shape your year
3. **Life chapters**: Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto transits shape your decades

If you're feeling a major shift you can't explain, look at Saturn and Pluto transits. If you're just having a weird Tuesday, it's probably the Moon.

## 3. The Most Important Transit Angles

A transit triggers a natal planet when it forms an aspect to it. The major aspects:

- **Conjunction (0°):** new cycle begins
- **Sextile (60°):** opportunity
- **Square (90°):** challenge, friction
- **Trine (120°):** ease, flow
- **Opposition (180°):** climax, decision point

The tighter the orb (within 1–2°), the stronger the transit.

## 4. Transit-to-House: The Life Area Activated

When a transiting planet moves through one of your 12 houses, it activates that life area:

- **Transiting Moon in your 4th house** — emotional focus on home and family today
- **Transiting Venus in your 7th house** — relationships feel warm and important this month
- **Transiting Saturn entering your 10th house** — a 2.5-year chapter of career restructuring begins

## 5. How to Read a Daily Transit Report

A good transit report shows three things per day:

1. **Which natal planet is being aspected** (what part of you is activated)
2. **Which transiting planet is making the aspect** (what kind of energy)
3. **Which house is being transited** (where in your life)

For example: *"Transiting Mars in your 10th house is squaring your natal Sun"* means: career pressure (Mars in 10th) is creating friction with your core identity (square to Sun) — likely felt as ambition clashing with fatigue.

## 6. Outer Planet Transits: The Long Arcs

The slow planets shape life chapters:

- **Saturn return (~age 28–30, 57–60):** the famous transition into adulthood or maturity
- **Uranus half-return (~age 40–42):** the midlife shake-up
- **Neptune square Neptune (~age 42):** the "what does it all mean?" phase
- **Pluto square Pluto (~age 36–40):** deep identity transformation

If you're going through a Saturn return right now, you're not crazy — the cosmos is restructuring your life.

## 7. How to Actually Use Transits

Transits are descriptive, not prescriptive. They describe *weather*, not destiny.

Practical uses:

- **Timing**: launch projects when Jupiter trines your Sun, not when Saturn squares it
- **Self-awareness**: understand why you're irritable (Mars transit) before snapping at loved ones
- **Patience**: when Saturn squares your Venus, your love life feels heavy — but it's also where real commitment gets tested
- **Permission**: when Jupiter crosses your Ascendant, it's okay to expand, take risks, say yes

## 8. A Word of Caution

Avoid "doom astrology" — forecasting worst-case outcomes from a transit. A Pluto transit is not destruction; it's transformation. A Saturn transit is not punishment; it's maturity. The language you use to read transits shapes how you experience them.

Track them, learn from them, but never let them run your life.
